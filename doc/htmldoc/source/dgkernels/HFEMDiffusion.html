      HFEMDiffusion

      Imposes the constraints on internal sides with HFEM.

      Overview

      This kernel applies the constraints on internal sides for diffusion equations with a hybrid finite element method (). Refer to DGKernels System for the full HFEM weak form for Poisson's equation.
